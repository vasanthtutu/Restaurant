SHORT STORY


Saint Louis is one of the best place to find Italian restaurant's. You can explore the Italian cuisine in the downtown.

Italian food is one of the most well known cuisines on the planet, and for good reason too. It's comforting, delicious,
and so satisfying. Whether you are craving a plate of pasta from Italy, a slice of Neapolitan pizza, a bite of creamy chicken,
or a bowl of warm and cozy soup, you will find it in the Italian kitchen. So if you are wondering how to make Italian food at
home for dinner, we have got you covered. We are sharing over 50 of our most popular and best Italian recipes including pasta,
pizza, soup, salad, chicken, beef, appetizers, bread, and more!
